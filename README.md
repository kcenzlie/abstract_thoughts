# Abstract Thoughts Release by kcenzlie

Hello, one of the soul-bearing beings on Earth. Welcome to the abstract depths of your inner self.

I know that deep down, you must have dreams of your own. No matter where you are on your journey—whether you’ve just set sail, are questioning its meaning, or have countless thoughts with no one to share them with—we understand.

Perhaps on a certain sleepless night, you desperately told yourself, I must create something that changes this world, or my life will lose its meaning. Perhaps in a crowd, you suddenly woke up as if from a dream, wondering why you were there and what any of those people had to do with you. Or maybe, on a solitary afternoon, you feverishly scrolled through the life story of an idol, feeling impossibly far from them, yet every step they took seemed like a fragment of your ideal life, leaving you longing.

If you’ve read this far, I want to tell you: Stop waiting. Stop wasting even one more second on hesitation and self-doubt. Nothing is worth trading a lifetime of potential for the false security of a world on the brink of the AGI era.

You define the life you want. If no one around you understands or listens to those wild yet brilliant ideas of yours, why not try this fully anonymous haven?

Others—like you or unlike you—will leave behind their advice, encouragement, and heartfelt words. You can also read the unspoken feelings of strangers, share your own experiences, and offer guidance.

This is a place of love and acceptance, where abstract, unfinished, or strange thoughts are refined into concrete steps forward. The advice you leave might help someone else in their darkest moments, filling this world with a little more warmth and beauty.

Every person’s potential is limitless, and our mission in this life is simply to become the brightest version of ourselves. ✨

# React + Vite

This template provides a minimal setup to get React working in Vite with HMR and some ESLint rules.

Currently, two official plugins are available:

- [@vitejs/plugin-react](https://github.com/vitejs/vite-plugin-react/blob/main/packages/plugin-react/README.md) uses [Babel](https://babeljs.io/) for Fast Refresh
- [@vitejs/plugin-react-swc](https://github.com/vitejs/vite-plugin-react-swc) uses [SWC](https://swc.rs/) for Fast Refresh

## Expanding the ESLint configuration

If you are developing a production application, we recommend using TypeScript and enable type-aware lint rules. Check out the [TS template](https://github.com/vitejs/vite/tree/main/packages/create-vite/template-react-ts) to integrate TypeScript and [`typescript-eslint`](https://typescript-eslint.io) in your project.
